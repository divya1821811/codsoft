SUSHANT SINGH RAJPUT

(21 January 1986 - 14 June 2020)

Biography

Sushant Singh Rajput was an Indian actor, dancer, and entrepreneur born on January 21, 1986, in Patna, Bihar, India.He was the youngest of five siblings and grew up in a middle-class family. Sushant completed his schooling from St. Karen's High School in Patna and pursued his higher education from Delhi Technological University (formerly Delhi College of Engineering). Sushant's interest in acting started during his college days when he joined Shiamak Davar's dance academy.

He then joined Barry John's theatre classes in Delhi and later moved to Mumbai to pursue a career in acting. In 2008, he was selected for the TV series "Kis Desh Mein Hai Meraa Dil," which marked his acting debut. However, he gained popularity with his role as Manav in the TV series "Pavitra Rishta," which aired from 2009 to 2011.


career

Sushant's acting career began with the TV series "Kis Desh Mein Hai Meraa Dil" in 2008. However, he gained popularity with his role as Manav in the TV series "Pavitra Rishta," which aired from 2009 to 2011. Sushant made his Bollywood debut in 2013 with the film "Kai Po Che!" directed by Abhishek Kapoor. He received critical acclaim for his performance and was nominated for the Filmfare Award for Best Male Debut. He then appeared in films like "Shuddh Desi Romance," "PK," "Detective Byomkesh Bakshy!," "M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story," and "Kedarnath," among others.


MOST SUCCESSFULL FILM





M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story is a biographical film directed by Neeraj Pandey, starring Sushant Singh Rajput as the lead character, Mahendra Singh Dhoni. The film portrays the inspiring journey of Dhoni, who rose from humble beginnings to become one of India's most celebrated cricket captains. The film begins with a young Dhoni, played by Rajput, working as a ticket collector at Kharagpur railway station in West Bengal.

Despite the difficulties he faced, Dhoni was determined to pursue his passion for cricket and worked hard to improve his skills. His dedication and perseverance paid off, and he was eventually selected for the Bihar under-19 team. The film then follows Dhoni's journey as he navigates the complex world of Indian cricket.

Despite facing opposition from those in power, he perseveres and eventually rises through the ranks to become the captain of the Indian cricket team. Along the way, he faces several challenges, both on and off the field, but he never loses sight of his goal. Sushant Singh Rajput's portrayal of Dhoni is remarkable, capturing the essence of the cricketer's humble yet determined persona.

The actor trained extensively to embody Dhoni's unique style of play, spending hours studying his body language and mannerisms. The film also features a talented ensemble cast, including Kiara Advani, Disha Patani, and Anupam Kher. Each actor delivers a powerful performance, bringing to life the people who played a significant role in Dhoni's life. The film is a visual treat, with stunning cinematography capturing the beauty of India's varied landscapes.

The soundtrack is equally impressive, featuring a blend of Indian and western music that perfectly captures the film's themes of tradition and modernity. Overall, M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story is a moving tribute to a remarkable Indian icon. Sushant Singh Rajput's performance is the highlight of the film, capturing the essence of Dhoni's inspiring journey. The film is a must-watch for cricket fans and anyone who values hard work, dedication, and perseverance.



DEATH


Sushant Singh Rajput, a talented Bollywood actor, passed away on June 14, 2020, at the age of 34. His sudden death came as a shock to the entire film industry and his fans around the world. According to reports, Sushant was found dead in his apartment in Bandra, Mumbai.

The police confirmed that he died by suicide. His death sparked a nationwide debate on mental health and the pressures of the film industry. Sushant's death left a void in the Indian film industry, and his fans were left devastated.The actor had a successful career in Bollywood, with notable performances in films like "Kai Po Che!," "M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story," "Chhichhore," and "Kedarnath."

Following his death, social media was flooded with messages of condolence and tributes from his colleagues and fans. Several Bollywood celebrities, including Akshay Kumar, Anushka Sharma, and Karan Johar, spoke out about the importance of mental health and the need to create a more supportive and inclusive film industry.
